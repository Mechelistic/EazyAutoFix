VEHICLE PARTS SALES

We do not repair our clients' vehicles with spare parts from roadside autoshops.

We have an understanding with Original Vehicle Manufacturers (OVM) - Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Mercedes Benz, etc. in the country to supply us with genuine spare parts of all models and types of their vehicles, at our request.

You are rest assured that replacement of your faulty vehicle spare parts would be with a "follow-come" (genuine) parts from the manufacturer of your vehicles.

We also supply genuine vehicle spare parts from the manufacturers to Service Providers who are not our members. All you need to do is to give us a call of fill the Spare Part Order Form with the Vehicle Type, Model and the Spare Part Part-No.

You are expected to pay into our Bank Account seventy percent (70%) of the spare part price and the remaining thirty percent (30%) paid on delivery of the part at the designated location, shipment fee inclusive.

We give you warantee as given to us by the OVM.

Due to liquidity in the prices of things, the price of every spare part requested by the client is given to him/her within an hour from the time of asking.
